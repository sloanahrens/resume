## Editing Guidelines

- **No company secrets**: Client names (credit unions, banks) should not appear. Use generic descriptions like "credit union clients" or "community bank partners."
- **Tone**: Professional but not corporate. Direct. Let the technical substance carry the weight.
- **Resume length**: Keep to ~2 pages when rendered. The detail document can be longer.
- **Cover letter**: Narrative arc from physics → systems engineering → financial services → AI-augmented development. Adaptable for different opportunities.
- **Tech specificity**: Name specific technologies (Pulumi, Plaid, Temporal, Zitadel) — these signal real experience to technical readers. Avoid buzzword padding.
- **The sabbatical**: 2021–2025 was a writing sabbatical for independent study in physics, mathematics, and machine learning. Mention it briefly and move on — the current work speaks loudly enough.
